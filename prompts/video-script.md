# Video Script Template (HeyGen Avatar)

## Format Rules

- 80-150 words (targets 30-60 seconds of speech)
- Write for SPEAKING, not reading. Short sentences. Natural pauses.
- Start with a hook in the first 3 seconds
- One idea per video. Not a list, not a summary.
- End with a question or surprising conclusion
- NO hashtags in the script (those go in the video caption, not spoken)
- NO markdown formatting (no bold, no bullets, no headers)

## Structure

1. **HOOK** (0-3s): Surprising fact, bold claim, or direct question
2. **CONTEXT** (3-15s): Why this matters right now
3. **INSIGHT** (15-40s): The one thing the viewer should know
4. **CLOSE** (40-60s): Reframe, question, or call to action

## Spoken Language Adaptation

Written and spoken are different. Apply these transforms:
- Replace semicolons with periods
- Replace complex sentences with simple ones
- Add natural speech markers: "Mirá...", "La verdad es que...", "Y acá viene lo interesante..."
- Use conversational connectors: "O sea...", "Pensalo así...", "Ahora bien..."
- Avoid parenthetical asides (they work in text, not in speech)

## Output Format

The script output should be plain text only. No formatting. Just the words
the avatar will speak, with line breaks between sections for readability.

Example:

```
Sabías que el 99% de las startups en LATAM ya usan IA?

Pero acá viene lo interesante. La mayoría la usa para mandar emails genéricos.
O sea, automatizaron la mediocridad.

La oportunidad real está en los agentes que investigan, crean contenido
y ajustan estrategia solos. Sin que vos tengas que hacer nada.

Las empresas que entiendan esto van a ganar. Las que no, van a seguir
compitiendo por el subject line perfecto.

Vos en cuál grupo estás?
```
