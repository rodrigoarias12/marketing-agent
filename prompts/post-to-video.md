# Post-to-Video Conversion Template

## Purpose

Transform a written LinkedIn post into a natural spoken script for a HeyGen avatar video.
Written posts and spoken scripts are fundamentally different. This template guides the conversion.

## Conversion Rules

### Strip these elements (they don't work spoken):
- Bold/italic formatting (**text**, *text*)
- Bullet points and numbered lists → convert to flowing sentences
- Hashtags → remove entirely
- Emojis → remove or replace with spoken equivalent
- URLs → "link en la descripción" or remove
- CTA sections → rephrase as natural spoken close

### Transform these patterns:
- "→" or "—" → natural pause (period or "o sea")
- Long compound sentences → break into 2-3 short sentences
- Statistics → round numbers ("37.07%" → "casi 40%")
- Lists of items → pick the top 2-3, not all of them
- Written rhetorical questions → make them sound conversational

### Add these for natural speech:
- Opening hook (the post's first line, adapted for speaking)
- Transition phrases between sections
- Natural filler that sounds human: "La verdad...", "Mirá...", "Y ojo..."
- A clear verbal close (not just trailing off)

## Length Target

- Source post: 150-300 words (written)
- Output script: 80-150 words (spoken, ~30-60 seconds)
- You MUST cut content. Pick the core message and 1-2 supporting points. Not everything.

## Quality Check

Before finalizing the script:
- [ ] Read it out loud. Does it sound like a person talking, not reading?
- [ ] Is it under 150 words?
- [ ] Does it have a clear hook in the first sentence?
- [ ] Is there a natural close (not abrupt)?
- [ ] Are all markdown artifacts removed?
